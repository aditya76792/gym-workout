Sunday — Full Rest
Gym: Closed for today
Sleep target: 8–9 hours
Rest is not optional — it's training. Muscles don't grow in the gym. They grow in the 48 hrs after, during recovery.
✓  Still hit 190g protein today — muscle doesn't know it's a rest day
✓  A 20–30 min walk is fine and actually speeds up recovery
✓  Sleep 8+ hours — peak growth hormone is released during deep sleep
✓  Foam roll or stretch if legs are sore from Saturday
Progressive overload: Every 2 weeks, increase weight by 2.5–5 kg on compound lifts (Bench, Squat, Deadlift, OHP) or add 1–2 reps per set. The moment it feels easy — it's time to add load.
